# Profile Kit Checklist

Ensure you have the following assets ready before applying:

- [ ] **Resume**: Updated and highlighting your 2+ years of QA experience.
- [ ] **Headshot**: Professional and clear.
- [ ] **Reel**: A short compilation of your best work (if applicable to the role).
- [ ] **Monologue/Intro Video**: A brief video introducing yourself, your experience, and what you bring to a team.

## Shooting Tips (for Intro Videos)
- **Lighting**: Face a window or use a ring light. Ensure your face is well-lit and avoid backlighting.
- **Background**: Keep it clean, simple, and uncluttered.
- **Audio**: Shoot in a quiet room. Use a lavalier mic or ensure your phone/camera is close enough to capture clear audio.
- **Framing**: Keep the camera at eye level. Frame yourself from the chest up.
- **Delivery**: Speak clearly, be confident, and keep it under 60 seconds.
